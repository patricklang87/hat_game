Use the controls at the bottom right to navigate to the astronaut below.

"Up" and "Down" allow you to squeeze between asteroids positioned diagonally to one another. "Jump" moves you to the right and allows you to spring over an asteroid. With "Hold", you stay still while the asteroids approach. With each move, the astroids come closer.
